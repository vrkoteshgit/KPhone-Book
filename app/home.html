Welcome Dashboard

  • Open Phone Book: Click on Pdanhone book menu in header to go to the phone book listing

  • Phone Book Contacts: List of contacts will appear with three buttons (View, Edit and Delete) for each contact

  • View Contact: Click on View button of the contact to view the contact details.
  • Edit Contact: Click on Edit button of the contact to edit. Edit data click on Update
  • Delete Contact: Click on the delete button to delete the selected contact

  • Create New Contact: Click on Create New and follow instructions to create new contact
  • Validation: Validation provided for all text input boxes, update and add button show hide based on form valid and dirty
